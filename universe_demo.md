# The Lattice Sea Chronicle (Demo Universe)

## Project Metadata
- Universe ID: `lattice-sea-demo`
- Genre: Science-fantasy
- Tone: Hopeful, mysterious, high-stakes exploration
- Era: Year 417 After the Fall

## Core Premise
After a planetary climate-collapse, surviving city-states migrated onto massive floating reef-plates called Lattices. Ancient biomechanical ruins beneath the ocean occasionally "wake," releasing artifacts that can heal ecosystems or destabilize gravity itself.

## Ontology Targets
- Species
- Locations
- Historical Events
- Entities (factions, organizations, relic systems)
- Individuals (characters)

## Species
### 1) Pelagons
- Type: Amphibious humanoids
- Traits: Bioluminescent veins, pressure-adapted lungs
- Culture: Oral legal traditions, memory-choruses
- Conflict: Distrust of surface-born scavenger guilds

### 2) Skyforged
- Type: Human descendants engineered for low-gravity rigs
- Traits: Hollow bone mesh, high vestibular tolerance
- Culture: Contractual honor and salvage law
- Conflict: Dependence on relic energy cores

### 3) Mosskin
- Type: Symbiotic human-plant lineages
- Traits: Photosynthetic skin patches, slow metabolic recovery
- Culture: Consensus councils and seed archives
- Conflict: Territorial disputes over freshwater condensers

## Locations
### 1) Veyra Spindle
- Kind: Vertical city built around a turbine shaft
- Importance: Trade nexus for oxygen cartridges
- Risk: Core turbine is 12% below safe resonance threshold

### 2) Blackglass Trench
- Kind: Abyssal excavation zone
- Importance: Primary relic recovery site
- Risk: Reality shear events during moon tides

### 3) The Hushed Canopy
- Kind: Dense floating forest biome
- Importance: Last known archive of pre-Fall pollen maps
- Risk: Bio-acoustic predators ("Chime Eels")

## Historical Events
### 1) The Fall (Year 0)
- Trigger: Cascading weather-engine failure
- Outcome: 68% landmass became uninhabitable

### 2) Concord of Nine Lattices (Year 121)
- Trigger: Resource wars over desalination towers
- Outcome: Shared navigation law and salvage treaties

### 3) Ember Flood (Year 389)
- Trigger: Malfunctioning relic reactor breach
- Outcome: Three Lattices evacuated; one remains quarantined

## Entities (Factions / Organizations / Systems)
### 1) Meridian Cartographers
- Type: Exploration guild
- Goal: Map stable routes through current anomalies
- Reputation: Reliable but politically neutral to a fault

### 2) The Salt Ledger
- Type: Banking and debt registry consortium
- Goal: Control trade credit between city-states
- Reputation: Efficient, predatory, unavoidable

### 3) Choir of Tides
- Type: Religious-scientific order
- Goal: Decode relic signals as "ocean scripture"
- Reputation: Brilliant, secretive, split by doctrine

### 4) Axiom Vault-9
- Type: Semi-sentient relic infrastructure
- Goal: Unknown; periodically issues navigation coordinates
- Reputation: Feared due to "correct but costly" predictions

## Individuals (Characters)
### 1) Captain Iri Vale
- Role: Skyforged salvage captain
- Motivation: Clear inherited family debt to the Salt Ledger
- Secret: Owns an illegal map to a sealed reactor nursery

### 2) Toman Reefspeaker
- Role: Pelagon diplomat and memory-chorus keeper
- Motivation: Prevent war between Veyra and Canopy settlements
- Secret: Suppressed evidence of treaty tampering

### 3) Sister Mael
- Role: Choir of Tides signal interpreter
- Motivation: Prove Axiom Vault-9 is communicating warnings, not commands
- Secret: Experiences shared dreams with unknown source

### 4) Dr. Sen Arclight
- Role: Mosskin ecologist
- Motivation: Restore extinct mangrove biomes on two Lattices
- Secret: Ember Flood may have been deliberate sabotage
